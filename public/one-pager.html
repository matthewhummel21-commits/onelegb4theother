🎖️ Who Qualifies

  • Any U.S. military veteran in need
  • Active duty transitioning out
  • Veterans experiencing hardship
  • Referred by a VSO, social worker, or community member
  • No income threshold required — we review each request personally

⚙️ How It Works

1
Scan or visit onelegb4theother.com and fill out a short request form
2
We review every request personally — usually within 48 hours
3
Approved gear ships directly to the veteran at no cost
4
We follow up — because this is a relationship, not a transaction
